Problem Identification

The core issue is the decentralized and informal nature of Bilkent's current mentorship resources, which fail to provide the proactive, personalized, and scalable support required by the large, diverse student body.

To validate the local scope of this problem, our team will conduct a quantitative Student Needs Assessment Survey to quantify anxiety levels, mentorship seeking behavior, and map the specific gaps in the existing system.

Comparative Benchmarking

We benchmark the existing Bilkent system against successful, technology-driven models at top peer institutions.

Critical Gap
  • Fragmented and low centralization
  • Department-specific programs (CTIS, POLS, Business only)
  • Inaccessible to majority of students
  • Relies on informal, high-commitment outreach

Identified Gap & Competitive Advantage

The crucial gap across all these models, which BUMS addresses, is the lack of a highly efficient, scalable system optimized for Bilkent-specific career/academic questions that requires Low (Micro-Mentoring) Commitment from busy alumni.

BUMS's Competitive Advantage: Efficiency and Specificity‚Äîachieving high matching precision with minimal time investment.

Proposed Solution: BUMS

A centralized, mobile-first application designed to address the time-commitment barrier and the need for precision matching.

Smart Matching Algorithm

Moves beyond simple major or sector matching. Leveraging Machine Learning (ML) principles, it matches users by:

  • Specific expertise
  • Stated needs
  • Self-reported anxiety levels
  • Learning from ASU's data-driven approach

  • Dramatically minimizes student waiting time

Feasibility Assessment

Timeline

Immediate Goal: Design a basic prototype within a four-week timeline, testing it with representative students.

Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4):

  • Concept Paper Approval
  • Problem Validation (Surveys/Interviews)
  • Prototype Design

Challenges & Mitigation

Primary Barrier: Mentor Recruitment and Retention

Mitigation Strategies:

  • Official University recognition and incentives (e.g., "Top Mentor" awards)
  • Reciprocal, high-value benefits for alumni
  • Premium access to searchable database of other BUMS mentors
  • Exclusive invitations to university-sponsored professional development seminars

Success Metrics

Immediate KPI:

  • Successful Heuristic Evaluation
  • Usability Testing completion

Long-Term KPIs:

  • Successful Match Rate: 75%+ of requests resulting in scheduled conversation
  • 15% increase in student-reported confidence regarding post-graduation planning within one academic year

Future Roadmap

  • Securing University funding
  • Minimum Viable Product (MVP) development
  • Pilot testing
  • Campus-wide rollout based on continuous user metrics
